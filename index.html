The Metropolitan Museum of Art

The Met presents over 5000 years of art from around the world for everyone to experience and enjoy.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met) is one of the largest and most prestigious art museums in the world, located in New York City. Founded in 1870, it houses over two million works of art spanning 5,000 years of human history, from ancient civilizations to contemporary art. The Met's collections are divided among three locations: The Met Fifth Avenue, The Met Breuer, and The Met Cloisters. The museum is renowned for its diverse exhibits, including European paintings, Egyptian artifacts, Asian art, and more. It's a cultural hub, offering educational programs, events, and exhibitions year-round.
